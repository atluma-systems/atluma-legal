Privacy Policy

Atluma  |  Effective date: March 27, 2026  |  Last updated: March 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Atluma collects, uses, and protects information when you use our app and services.

1. Information We Collect

Account information. When you create an account, we collect your email address and a password (stored as a one-way hash — we never store your plain-text password). You may optionally provide a display name.

User-submitted content. When you submit content through the app, we store the text you provide along with associated metadata such as date and time. Your content is private by default and is not shared with other users without your explicit consent.

AI processing. When you submit content, we send it to third-party AI providers (currently OpenAI) to generate structured analysis and insights. This processing happens automatically as part of the core service. AI providers process your content under their own privacy and data processing agreements. We do not permit AI providers to use your content to train their models.

Derived data. We generate mathematical representations (embeddings) of your submitted content to enable search and, where you have consented, analysis across our platform. These representations do not contain your original text.

Usage data. We collect basic usage information such as which features you use, app crashes, and performance data to help us improve the product. We do not sell this data or use it for advertising.

Device information. We collect your device type and operating system version for compatibility and debugging purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the Atluma service
  • To process your submitted content and generate AI-powered insights
  • To enable personal discovery features within your own account
  • To enable collective analysis across contributed content (only with your explicit consent — see Section 3)
  • To send you optional reminders and notifications you configure
  • To respond to support requests
  • To improve the platform through aggregated, anonymized usage analysis

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not share your submitted content with other users without your explicit consent.

3. Your Consent Choices

You control how your submitted content is used. Each item of content is either Private (visible only to you, never used for collective analysis) or Contributed (included in platform-wide analysis). Private is the default. You can change this setting at any time.

For contributed content, you may choose to participate anonymously or with your identity attached. Anonymous is the default. Downgrading your consent level is always permitted and will be honored promptly.

4. Data Sharing

AI providers. We share submitted content text with OpenAI for processing. This is governed by OpenAI's data processing agreement. We opt out of model training where that option is available.

Infrastructure providers. We use Supabase for database hosting and authentication. Your data is stored on Supabase's infrastructure under their data processing terms.

Research partners. In the future, Atluma may work with academic research institutions. Only content you have explicitly marked as Contributed will ever be considered for research use, and only under appropriate consent documentation. We will notify you before any such partnership affects your data.

Legal requirements. We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, or other legal process — but we will notify you unless legally prohibited from doing so.

We do not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or marketing companies.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your account and submitted content are retained for as long as your account is active. You may delete individual items at any time from within the app. You may request deletion of your entire account and all associated data by contacting us at privacy@atluma.io — we will complete deletion within 30 days.

Content you have contributed to collective analysis may persist in anonymized, aggregated form after account deletion, consistent with the consent terms under which it was contributed.

6. Security

We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), encrypted storage for sensitive fields, and row-level security on our database. Passwords are hashed and are never stored in plain text. We conduct periodic security reviews.

No system is perfectly secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please contact us at security@atluma.io.

7. Children's Privacy

Atluma is intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable privacy law (including GDPR and CCPA) to access, correct, port, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@atluma.io. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as Atluma evolves. If we make material changes, we will notify you via email or a prominent notice in the app before the changes take effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
